Why HerRelief exists

HerRelief started with a simple frustration: why does managing period pain still mean choosing between drowsy painkillers, messy patches, or burning your lap with a fixed heating pad?

We spent months researching, testing, and listening to women about what they actually needed — something wearable, wireless, and discreet enough to wear through a work meeting or a yoga class. Something that gives real, targeted heat exactly where the cramps hit, without a single pill.

The result is the HerRelief heating massage belt — a drug-free, reusable belt with 5 heat settings and vibration massage, designed to wrap around your lower abdomen and get to work in minutes.
